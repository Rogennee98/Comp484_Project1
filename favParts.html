Episode 151, "A Conversation With A Barber, During A Haircut, Is The Most Pointless Thing In The World "

The odd jobs were trying to help out the barber, as he left to get a manga that he thought he didnt have. The odd jobs were hoping no one would come to the barber shop as they had no idea how to cut other peoples hair. Just as they were hoping that no one was going to come in the barber, a policeman and a joi rebel came in. And just to make matters worst the shogun also took a visit to get his haircut.

Episode 119, "Within Each Box Of Cigarettes, Are One Or Two Cigarettes That Smell Like Horse Dung"

There was a ban in smoking in the community. This was a problem for Toshiro as he was addicted to somking. He tried to quit, but he couldnt handle the addiction so he decided to go travel to a different planet to find a cigarette and have a smoke. The problem was the other planets also were out of cigarette, because a bunch of bandits took all the cigarettes and ran sacked the city that made the cigarettes . So Toshiro went on a mission to find the bandits and save the city that makes the cigarettes. But in order to save the city and find his cigarettes he needs to find all of the ooy gooey balls and take them to the dragon to make his wish come true.
